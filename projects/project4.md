# Project 4: Job Packet

## Overview

In this assignment, you will create a **professional job application packet** tailored to a specific position in your field of interest.

You will research a real job posting and produce materials that demonstrate your ability to:

* Present your skills and experience clearly
* Adapt your writing to a specific audience
* Communicate professionally in a workplace context

This project connects your technical writing skills to real-world career preparation.

## Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment, you will:

* Write professional documents for a specific audience and purpose
* Analyze job postings and employer expectations
* Develop a clear, effective resume
* Write a targeted cover letter
* Reflect on how your skills align with industry needs

## Assignment Description

You will locate a **real job or internship posting** related to your field (or a field you want to enter).

Using that posting, you will create a complete job packet that includes:

1. A tailored **resume**
2. A targeted **cover letter**
3. A short **job analysis** explaining your choices

## Required Components

### 1. Job Posting

Include a link or copy of the job posting you are responding to.

The position should:

* Be realistic for your current or near-future qualifications
* Clearly list responsibilities and required skills

### 2. Resume

Your resume should:

* Be **1 page** (recommended)
* Be clearly formatted and easy to scan
* Highlight relevant skills, education, and experience
* Use concise bullet points and action verbs
* Be tailored to the selected job

### 3. Cover Letter

Your cover letter should:

* Be addressed to a specific company or role
* Introduce who you are and what position you are applying for
* Highlight 2–3 key qualifications
* Demonstrate knowledge of the company or role
* Maintain a professional tone and format

### 4. Job Analysis (Reflection)

Write a **short analysis** explaining:

* Why you chose this job
* What skills and qualifications the employer is seeking
* How you tailored your resume and cover letter
* Where you are strong and where you need improvement

## Formatting & Submission

* Resume: PDF, 1 page (2 max)
* Cover Letter: PDF, 200-250 words
* Job analysis: Google Doc, 200-250 words

## Audience Requirement

Your audience includes:

* Hiring managers
* Recruiters
* Industry professionals

Your documents should reflect professional expectations in tone, clarity, and formatting.

## Process & Deadlines

* **Job Posting + Proposal:** (due Week 13)
* **Draft Materials:** (due Week 14)
* **Final Submission:** (due Week 14)

Revision is expected after feedback.

## Evaluation Criteria

Your work will be evaluated based on:

* **Resume Quality** – Clarity, relevance, formatting, conciseness
* **Cover Letter Effectiveness** – Audience awareness, specificity, tone
* **Tailoring to Job Posting** – Alignment with employer needs
* **Job Analysis** – Thoughtful reflection and clarity
* **Professionalism** – Presentation, style, correctness
* **Technical Execution** – Organization and GitHub submission

## Tips for Success

* Customize everything—avoid generic applications
* Use language from the job posting strategically
* Focus on results and skills, not just duties
* Keep formatting clean and consistent
* Be honest, but present yourself confidently

## Note

This assignment is about job preparation and practice, not perfection. You are not expected to have extensive experience. Instead, you should focus on clearly representing the skills you *do* have.
